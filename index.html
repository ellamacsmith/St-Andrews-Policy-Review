Submissions

Submit an Article

We welcome submissions from students, academics, and policy professionals on topics related to politics, public affairs, and policy. All submissions are reviewed by our editorial board prior to publication.

Please send your article to:

Submissions email

standrewspolicyreview@gmail.com

Submission Guidelines

Citation Style

Use Chicago Style — footnotes or endnotes are both accepted.

Format

Submit as a Word document (.doc or .docx).

Font

Use Size 12 in a legible font such as Times New Roman or Garamond.

File Name

Use your first initial and last name, followed by "StAPR" and a short keyword from your article title or type.

ESmith_StAPR_Democracy.docx PRhodes_StAPR_OpEd1.docx  (if submitting more than one op-ed)
Author Info

Include your name as you would like it to appear, along with the article title, at the top of the document. You may submit anonymously — please specify this in your email if so.

Article Type

At the top of the document, specify the type of article — e.g., Op-Ed, Policy Analysis, Research Essay, Review.

Who We Are

About

St Andrews Policy Review is a nonpartisan student-led journal committed to publishing thoughtful, well-researched commentary on politics, policy, and public affairs. We elevate emerging voices in academia and journalism to foster meaningful discourse.

The views expressed in published articles are solely those of the authors and do not reflect the positions of the editorial board.
